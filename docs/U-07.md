# U-07 Remove Unnecessary Accounts

## Overview
This check verifies that unnecessary accounts are removed from the system.

## Purpose
To reduce the risk of unauthorized access by eliminating unused,
default, or dormant accounts.

## Check Files
- /etc/passwd

## Commands
- cat /etc/passwd
- last

## Good Condition
No unnecessary accounts exist.

## Vulnerable Condition
Unnecessary accounts exist.

## Evidence
- Full account list
- Default account presence
- Login history
- Identified inactive accounts

## Risk
Unnecessary accounts increase the risk of unauthorized access,
account misuse, and exploitation of dormant accounts.

## Remediation
1. Identify unnecessary accounts using /etc/passwd.
2. Check login history using `last`.
3. Remove unused accounts using:
   userdel <username>
4. Verify system impact before removing system accounts.

## Root Privilege
Partial required
