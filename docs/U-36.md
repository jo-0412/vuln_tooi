# U-36 Disable r Services

## 1. Overview

- **Item Code**: U-36
- **Item Name**: Disable r Services
- **Severity**: High
- **Category**: UNIX > Service Management

This item checks whether legacy r services such as `rsh`, `rlogin`, and `rexec` are disabled.

---

## 2. Purpose

The purpose of this check is to prevent unauthorized access and privilege abuse
caused by weak trust-based remote access services.

r services were designed for trusted environments and may rely on host-based trust
relationships rather than strong authentication.

---

## 3. Files to Check

- `/etc/inetd.conf`
- `/etc/xinetd.d/*`
- `/etc/hosts.equiv`
- `$HOME/.rhosts`

---

## 4. Commands to Check

```bash
grep -Ei 'rsh|rlogin|rexec' /etc/inetd.conf
grep -RinE 'disable|rsh|rlogin|rexec' /etc/xinetd.d/
systemctl list-units --type=service | grep -E 'rlogin|rsh|rexec'
find /home -name .rhosts -o -name hosts.equiv 2>/dev/null
